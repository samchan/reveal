🐣 Junior Chikin's Gender Reveal 🍼

Crack the code from these 4 riddles to reveal the baby’s gender!

🧒 Riddle One

Before I can walk, I learn to babble,
With shaky steps and tiny scrabble.
A single candle marks my start,
Of first real words and growing heart.
What number marks this tender year,
When “mama” or “ball” you first might hear?

Hint: “It’s the age when most first words are spoken.”

🍗 Riddle Two

A family feast with sides and spice,
You reach the tray — oh, wouldn’t it be nice.
But all that’s left is bone and box,
The meat is gone, like vanished socks.
In pieces left for you to score,
Count what’s there… or maybe less than more.

Hint: “You were too slow — and they were hungry.”

👟 Riddle Three

You point and grin, no bib in sight,
Then blurt a word in pure delight.
Not quite “chicken,” but close enough —
Your first food word, a little rough.
Of all the things you’ve ever said,
How many fried treats filled your thread?

Hint: “The number of fried chicken pieces in your very first food memory.”

🗣️ Riddle Four

By now your words flow clear and fast,
The lisps and blends are in the past.
You tell full tales with plot and flair,
With jokes and rhymes and wild fanfare.
At what age do most experts rate,
That all speech sounds should articulate?

Hint: “It’s the upper age limit for sound development milestones in SLP.”

👦 Junior Chikin is a boy! 👦
